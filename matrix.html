📌
Footnotes ① ② VA does not technically allow financing discount points on a Cash Out — however, if the borrower is receiving funds back, the cash back will be reduced by the amount of the discount points.
③ 100% LTV if discount points ≤ 1%  |  90% LTV if discount points > 1%
ℹ️
IRRRL Fixed → ARM Appraisal Note Per VA Circular 26-19-22, a new appraisal is needed on Fixed-to-ARM IRRRLs to determine LTV — particularly when discount points are involved and LTV must be verified. Confirm appraisal requirement with your investor on these transactions.
ℹ️
Seasoning — 210 Days: VA vs. FHA For VA loans, the 210 days is measured from the first payment due date of the loan being refinanced. For FHA Streamlines, the 210 days is measured from the closing date of the loan being refinanced (case number cannot be requested until the 211th day from closing). Both also require 6 consecutive payments.
⚠️
Texas VA Loans You cannot pull cash out with a Government loan in Texas. Use Conventional only. Since you can't finance discount points on a TX Cash Out, if the borrower is disqualified from an IRRRL, they must bring discount points to closing.
ℹ️
Streamlines You can only do a streamline if it's VA to VA (or FHA to FHA). Conv to Gov and Gov to Conv are always full document loans.
