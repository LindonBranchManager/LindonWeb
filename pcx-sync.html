Common Questions

How often do I need to click the bookmark?
PopCX tokens expire roughly every 12 hours. The Lead Pipeline will show a red "Token expired" warning when it needs refreshing. When you see that, just go to PopCX and click your bookmark — takes 3 seconds. Most people will do this once in the morning.
What if the bookmark stops working?
Make sure you're on a PopCX page (popcx.com) when you click it — it won't work from other sites. If it still doesn't work, come back here and re-drag the button to replace the old bookmark.
Is my PopCX password being stored anywhere?
No. The bookmark never touches your password. It only copies a temporary access token — the same kind of short-lived key that PopCX already uses internally when you're logged in. It expires automatically and is only stored in your browser.
I dragged it but I don't see it in my bookmarks bar
The bookmarks bar may be hidden. Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to show it, then try dragging again. If the bar is full, right-click any existing bookmark and delete one to make space.
