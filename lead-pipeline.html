How to get your PopCX token:
  1. Log into popcx.com in Chrome
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Click Network tab → filter by Fetch/XHR
  4. Click any XHR request → Headers → find Authorization
  5. Copy everything after "Bearer " — that's your token
⏱ Tokens expire after ~12 hours. You'll need to refresh when it stops working.
